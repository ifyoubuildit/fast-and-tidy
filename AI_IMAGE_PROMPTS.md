## Best AI Tools to Use:

1. **Midjourney** (Discord bot) - Highest quality
2. **DALL-E 3** (via ChatGPT Plus) - Very good, easy to use
3. **Stable Diffusion** (ComfyUI/Automatic1111) - Free but more complex
4. **Leonardo.ai** - Good free tier
5. **Firefly** (Adobe) - Good for commercial use

## Tips for Best Results:

- Use "photorealistic" and "4K resolution" in every prompt
- Specify "natural lighting" or "daylight"
- Add "high detail" for better quality
- For items, always specify "PNG with transparent background"
- Generate multiple versions and pick the best
- Keep the same room layout between messy/clean versions

## Image Specifications:

- **Room backgrounds**: 1920x1080px (landscape)
- **Individual items**: 200x200px max (PNG with transparency)
- **File format**: JPG for backgrounds, PNG for items
- **Quality**: High resolution for crisp display on all devices
